Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Zamah Expense Tracker, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Zamah Expense Tracker service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs based on usage patterns

We will never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers located in India. We apply industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is restricted to authorised personnel only. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us — deletion is processed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Zamah Expense Tracker does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use the following categories of third-party services to operate the app:

  • Hosting providers: to serve the application and store data
  • Analytics tools: to understand aggregate usage patterns (no personally identifiable data shared)

Any third-party providers we use are contractually bound to handle your data in accordance with this policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the Zamah Expense Tracker support channel. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
